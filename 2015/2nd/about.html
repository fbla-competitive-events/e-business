About Us

This site was designed to give young artistic entrepreneurs an opportunity to showcase their art online to both sell art and gain followers and popularity. The main goal is to spread the word about up and coming artists to help promote their art and improve business. The store is solely online, and is not a brick-and-mortar establishment Here at Couey Corp® we aim to provide customers with a clean, professional, yet engaging website to make the viewer experience as pleasing as possible. We hope that the site will benefit the careers of these artists and help them on their track to becoming professionals. Couey Corp ® was founded by LaShawn Couey in 2013 as a means to bring in business for web designers as well as provide affordable and professional designs for customers to help promote them and increase their business. LaShawn is currently the CEO and head designer and hopes to continue to grow his company and provide his services to businesses in need as well as charities, individuals, and foundations.

Qualifications

You cannot be an experienced or professional artist. You cannot have an established business. This site is designed for artists recently coming onto the scene to showcase their work. Its purpose is to increase exposure for new or part time artists. If you think you meet the qualifications fill out this form and we'll review your submission for placement on the site. We accept all kinds of art. All you have to do is fill out the form under the "Submit Your Art" button and attach an image of your art. We'll get back to you as soon as we can.
